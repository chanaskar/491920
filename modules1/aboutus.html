Karthikeyan Narayanasamy

Chief Executive Officer

Karthikeyan hails from a small village named Koviloor , Thanjavur District, South India . His father was a farmer and had different type of plantations across Tamilnadu .However for Karthik his legacy was a stepping stone to become an far reached entrepreneur with diversified interest. With basic primary education in academics he moved out of his hometown to Singapore in the year 1998 to look out for a living but as the saying goes his destiny was Dubai, UAE. He started off being a mere structured cabling technician part of the Al-Ghurair Group. He went on to found COMMNET , a strong IT Services Consulting company headquartered in Dubai ,with branches across GCC , India & Singapore. Today he is the Chairman & CEO of Commnet Group (Commnet Systems Consultancy LLC, Commnet Systems Consultancy India Pvt Ltd, Commnet Distribution Pvt LTD & Commnet Systems Consultancy Pte ltd )
